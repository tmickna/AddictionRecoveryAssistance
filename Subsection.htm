Alcoholism is a condition that affects millions of individuals. A formal definition would describe alcolism as "The inability to control drinking due to both a physical and emotional dependence on alcohol." A clinical definition does not accurately and completely describe this disease as it can manifest itself quite differently between those who suffer from it.

Recently, the term "Alcoholism", has been changed to "Alcohol Use Disorder".
