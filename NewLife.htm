Rarely does the recovery process involve simply stopping. It’s necessary to create a new life for yourself. A life where it’s easier for you to not use. Without taking this step, all the factors that started your addiction will continue to surround you and will make the recovery process more difficult.

You don’t have to change everything about your life. And certainly not all at once. It can be difficult letting go of your old life in favor of a new one but it's absolutely necessary. It's best to begin slowly until the process becomes more comfortable. Believe it or not, once you've begun making changes, it will become a part of you. You'll begin to see the things that need changing more easily, you'll start to feel better about yourself and your life will make you happy again.
